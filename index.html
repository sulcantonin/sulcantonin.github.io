About

I am a Researcher at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab (LBNL) specializing in autonomous systems. Currently, my work focuses on developing intelligent algorithms for particle accelerator controls, bridging the gap between advanced AI and high-energy physics. Previously a Senior Data Scientist at the Helmholtz Association (DESY/XFEL), I pioneered the use of Large Language Models in scientific contexts, including the development of "PACuna" and "ChatQCD". I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Vision from the University of Konstanz and actively shape the field as a Program Chair of Various Conferences & Workshops.
