About

I specialize in developing AI solutions for complex scientific problems, particularly in physics and particle accelerators. With a Ph.D. in Computer Vision from Uni Konstanz and extensive experience in machine learning, I bridge the gap between academic research and practical applications.

Currently working at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, I focus on developing innovative AI solutions for particle accelerator control and optimization, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in scientific computing.
